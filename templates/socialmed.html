SOCIAL MEDIA USAGE ANALYSIS

Social media has profoundly transformed modern society,shaping how we communicate, access information, and perceive the world .It facilitates instant connectivity, allowing people to maintain relationships across great distances and enabling the rapid dissemination of news and ideas. However, this pervasive influence comes with significant drawbacks, including the spread of misinformation, the erosion of privacy, and the potential for heightened anxiety and depression due to online comparison and cyberbullying.

This application aims at predicting the emotion of a user influenced by interation with different social media platforms based on some entities.
